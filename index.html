How It Works

Here, we create a variety of designs, and we’ll give a quick explanation of how we build UX/UI interfaces

Discovery & Direction

We define the goals of the website’s look and the client’s design, set the required timeframe, clarify all the details, and turn their ideas into reality

Code & Creation

The codes are built from scratch, and the work is done either on Visual Studio Code or Notepad++, there’s no difference

Project and Delivery

The project is delivered in full, including all the codes, after defining the project’s structure, type, and other details

Prototype & Review

The client is provided with a trial version of the website (the pre-final version) to test it and give their feedback. This stage aims to ensure that the website is free of technical errors and that it meets the client’s requirements, while also allowing room for necessary adjustments before the final delivery

Handoff & Launch

A clean design according to the client’s preferences and within the required timeframe
